Spotlight · Kotak Mahindra Bank · 2021–2025

Smart Nudge Platform

1

The Brief

I joined Kotak's Innovation Labs with a clear brief: improve click-through rates on customer nudges.

2

The Real Problem

CTR turned out to be the visible issue, not the root problem. More than 20 teams across the bank were sending communications through different vendors, with different tools, tone, and disconnected data. Customers received duplicate messages, consent was not managed in one place, reporting was patchy, and there was no real feedback loop. The bank didn't just need better messaging. It needed a shared platform.

3

What I Built

I proposed a bank-wide communication platform: one egress layer for every customer touchpoint across five channels. I defined the strategy, set the metrics, built the roadmap, and helped take it from idea to production over three years. I worked with each line of business, wrote PRDs, onboarded use cases, and pushed migrations through. In the end, 20+ communication teams became 2, marketing costs came down, and 51 million customers got a more consistent, consent-driven experience with lifecycle journeys and fallback rules.
