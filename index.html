Spotlight · Kotak Mahindra Bank · 2021–2025

Smart Nudge Platform

1

The Brief

I joined Kotak's Innovation Labs with a straightforward ask — improve nudge CTR for banking customers. Make people click on the messages the bank sends them.

2

The Real Problem

But the CTR problem was a symptom, not the disease. I found 20+ teams across the bank sending communications through different vendors, each with their own tools, tone, and data silos. Customers got duplicate messages across channels. There was no unified consent, no delivery reports, no feedback loop. The bank was spending heavily on fragmented, sub-optimal tooling when it needed one cohesive platform.

3

What I Built

I pitched a product vision to build a bank-wide communication platform — a single egress point for every customer touchpoint across 5 channels. I defined the strategy, mapped success metrics, built the roadmap, and led the team from concept to production over 3 years. I personally went to every LOB, wrote their PRDs, onboarded their use cases, and convinced them to migrate. We reduced 20+ communication teams to 2, cut marketing cost, and gave 51 million customers a unified, consent-driven experience with lifecycle journeys and smart fallback rules.
